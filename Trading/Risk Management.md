# Risk Management — Sith Capital Preservation Rules

**Tags:** #trading #sith

> *"I am altering the deal. Pray I don't alter it any further."* — Never let the market say this to you.

---

## Core Rules

| Rule | Parameter |
| :--- | :--- |
| Max risk per trade | 1% of total capital |
| Max daily drawdown | 3% of total capital |
| Max open positions | 5 concurrent |
| Stop loss | Always set before entry |
| Position sizing | Risk ÷ (Entry − Stop) |

## Drawdown Protocol

- **-3% day:** Stop trading. Review journal. Resume tomorrow.
- **-10% week:** Reduce size by 50% for the following week.
- **-20% month:** Full stop. Strategy review required.

## The Sith Rule of Risk

*Preserve capital above all. The dark side is patient. Opportunities return. Capital, once lost, must be earned back twice.*
