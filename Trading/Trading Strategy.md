## Core Philosophy

The dark side of trading is patience, data, and ruthless execution. LORD VADER (@SithTrades1) does not trade on hope. Every position is backed by signal, sized by risk, and exited by plan.

## Framework

1. **Signal First** — No entry without a confirmed signal from [[Trading/Market Signals]]
2. **Size by Risk** — See [[Trading/Risk Management]] for position sizing rules
3. **No Revenge Trading** — A loss is data. Process it. Move on.
4. **Let Winners Run** — The dark side does not cut winners short.
5. **Auto-Document** — Every trade logged in [[Journal/Daily Journal]]

## Edge Sources

- Momentum + mean reversion hybrid
- Volume profile analysis
- Macro regime awareness
- Sentiment divergence signals
