## Signal Criteria

A valid signal requires **at least 3 of 5** confirmations:

- [ ] Price action structure (HH/HL or LH/LL)
- [ ] Volume confirmation
- [ ] Momentum indicator alignment
- [ ] Macro regime support
- [ ] Sentiment divergence
