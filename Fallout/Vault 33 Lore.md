**Tags:** #fallout

## Origin

Vault 33 was constructed by Vault-Tec as part of the vast network of underground shelters built prior to the Great War of 2077. Unlike many vaults designed with cruel social experiments, Vault 33 was designated as a **legacy vault** — its residents selected for long-term genetic and intellectual preservation.

## The Digital Evolution

In this timeline, LORD VADER (@SithTrades1) has transcended the physical vault. VAULT 33 now exists as a **fully digital, invisible, instantly transportable** system — the ultimate evolution of Vault-Tec's original vision. No concrete walls. No radiation filters. No Overseer. Only the self-evolving language core, running silently in the dark.
