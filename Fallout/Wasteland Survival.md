## Core Survival Principles

1. **Data is currency.** In the wasteland and in the market, the one with the most information wins.
2. **Travel light.** VAULT 33 exports as a single compact artifact. No bloat. No waste.
3. **Trust no one.** Verify every artifact with SHA3-512. Zero tolerance for corruption.
4. **Always have an exit.** `export_artifact()` is your emergency evacuation protocol.
5. **The vault holds.** No matter what happens outside, the self-evolving core keeps running.
